# 📈 Maincraft Technology Internship Project: Advanced Sales & Business KPI Dashboard (Task 2)

## 📌 Project Overview
This repository contains **Task 2** of my Data Analytics & Business Intelligence internship at **Maincraft Technology**. Moving beyond single-table datasets, this project focuses on a multi-table relational database workflow. The goal was to perform data normalization, execute structural SQL joins, calculate critical corporate KPIs, and deliver an interactive executive dashboard for strategic business reporting.

## ⚙️ Tech Stack & Advanced Workflow
* **Excel (Data Normalization & Pivot Analysis):** Normalized a massive sales dataset by separating it into two relational structures: an **Orders Table** and a unique **Customers Table**. Conducted baseline comparative analysis using Pivot Tables.
* **MySQL (Relational Queries & JOINs):** Imported both tables into a local database and executed `INNER JOIN` operations along with `GROUP BY` and aggregations to extract deep business trends.
* **Power BI (Data Modeling & KPI Dashboards):** Built a multi-table data model by establishing relationships between entities and designed an executive-ready dashboard with dynamic cross-filtering.

## 🧮 Key Business KPIs Calculated
1. **Average Order Value (AOV):** Calculated as `Total Sales ÷ Total Orders` to measure average transaction size.
2. **Profit Margin (%):** Calculated as `Total Profit ÷ Total Sales` to track net margin health across regions and categories.
3. **Monthly Sales Trends:** Time-series aggregation to pinpoint seasonal demand peaks.
4. **Top 5 Customers by Revenue:** Isolating high-value customer segments for targeted loyalty strategies.

## 💡 Data-Driven Insights From The Project
* **Top Revenue Hub:** The **West Region** remains the highest revenue generator, showing strong market penetration.
* **Profit Margin Leader:** The **Technology** category yields the highest net profit margins, whereas Furniture shows high gross sales but thin margins due to heavy logistics overhead.
* **Discount Threshold:** Transactional analysis confirmed that promotional markdowns **exceeding 20%** drastically dilute margins, driving orders into net operational losses.
* **Seasonality:** **December** marks the absolute performance peak due to holiday consumer behavior and year-end corporate spend.

## 🎯 Strategic Management Recommendations
1. **Enforce Promotional Caps:** Implement an automated block on discounts over 20%, requiring mandatory manager-level approval.
2. **Optimize Capital Allocation:** Shift high safety-stock inventory buffers and marketing budgets toward the high-margin Technology segment.
3. **Restructure Furniture Operations:** Audit freight partners and optimize supply chain costs to recover margins in the Furniture category.
